The Best Movies Or Series According to Frankie

My top 3 movies or series of all time.

From

"From" is a sci-fi horror series that follows the residents of a mysterious town that traps everyone who enters. The show centers on a group of people, including a family, who find themselves stranded in this eerie place, where they encounter strange creatures that emerge at night. As they struggle to survive and uncover the town's secrets, they grapple with the psychological toll of their situation and the complexities of human relationships. The series explores themes of fear, survival, and the unknown, blending suspense with supernatural elements.

The Walking Dead

"The Walking Dead" is a post-apocalyptic horror series that follows a group of survivors navigating a world overrun by zombies, known as "walkers." The story begins with sheriff's deputy Rick Grimes waking from a coma to find society collapsed. He joins a diverse group of survivors, including family and friends, as they face not only the threat of the undead but also the challenges of human conflict and moral dilemmas. The series delves into themes of survival, community, and the human condition, exploring how individuals change in the face of relentless danger and loss.

The Men Who Built America

"The Men Who Built America" is a historical documentary miniseries that chronicles the rise of influential American industrialists in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. It focuses on key figures such as John D. Rockefeller, Andrew Carnegie, Cornelius Vanderbilt, and J.P. Morgan, highlighting their contributions to the nation's economic development and the impact of their rivalries. The series explores themes of ambition, innovation, and the complexities of capitalism, illustrating how these men shaped modern America through their business practices, personal struggles, and social dynamics during a transformative period in history.
