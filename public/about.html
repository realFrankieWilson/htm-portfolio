About Me

Hey there! I’m Frank, but you can call me Frankie—your friendly neighborhood curious mind!

Hailing from a quaint village in Nigeria, I’ve always had a knack for uncovering the mysteries of how things tick. My insatiable curiosity often led me down a path of exploration (and a few mishaps) as I’d take things apart, only to find myself scratching my head over how to piece them back together.

This adventurous spirit is what ignited my passion for becoming an electrician. Under the guidance of an incredible mentor, I dove deep into the world of electrical installations, maintenance, and troubleshooting. Each spark of knowledge fueled my desire to learn more!

But why stop there? That same curiosity has now propelled me into the exciting realm of programming! It’s been an exhilarating journey where I not only get to decode the behavior of computers but also write my own commands and bring ideas to life through code.

So, join me as I share my unique perspective as an electrician turned programmer. Together, we’ll explore innovative projects, tackle challenges, and discover the fascinating intersection of electrical engineering and coding! Feel free to check out my work on GitHub here.
